Activities at the Pacific Trails Resort

Hiking

Pacific Trail Resort has 5 miles of hiking trails adjacent to a state park. Go it alone or join one of our guided hikes.

Kayaking

Ocean kayaks are available for guests to use.

Bird Watching

While anytime is a good time for bird watching at Pacific Trails, we offer guided birdwatching trips at sunrise several times a week.
